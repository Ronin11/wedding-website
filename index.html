Our Timeline

Nate first saw April in the Fall of 2015 when she moved in upstairs. She really impressed him when she came downstairs and asked if she could borrow an angle grinder to sharpen the lawn mower blades.

April thought Nate was cool before she met him, because he had a Serenity sticker on his car.

They really started to hit it off the Summer of 2016 when they went through rabies shots, joined a D&D campaign, built a pergola, and dug a koi pond together.

In Spring of 2017 they moved in together. Later that year they bought a house and moved to South Jordan.

September 20th 2020, April and Nate were married at Lake Mary up Big Cottonwood Canyon, Utah.
